Bart's Burrito Bonanza

Ingredients

  • 1 can (16 ounces) refried beans
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1 cup cooked long grain rice
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese, divided
  • 12 flour tortillas (6 inches)
  • Shredded lettuce, optional

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°. In a large bowl, combine beans, salsa, rice and 1 cup cheese. Spoon about 1/3 cup off-center on each tortilla. Fold the sides and ends over filling and roll up.
  2. Arrange burritos in a greased 13x9-in. baking dish. Sprinkle with remaining 1 cup cheese. Cover and bake until heated through, 20-25 minutes. If desired, topped with lettuce.
